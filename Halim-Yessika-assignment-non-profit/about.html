Meet the Team

We are an Indonesian Christian Youth Group from San Jose, California, USA. As young ambassadors of Jesus Christ, our aim is to praise the Lord through volunteering and other creative ways. We hope to make a difference in our community and also apply the various skills acquired to represent and innovate Indonesia's housing environment. By embodying the Core Value of "Family" in everything that we do, we believe that we can be the bridge between the Indonesian and American community. God bless!

Cross-cultural Research Project: Adapting U.S. Sustainable Design Work to Indonesian contexts

Our research project focuses on gathering information and evidence on living conditions and environmental situations in well-developed cities such as Bali, Jakarta, and Yogyakarta as well as poor communities like Bandung, and Pontianak. Through immersion with the community, we examine the similarities and differences of both lifestyles (e.g. climate changes, accessibility to stores, soil gradation, etc.) to improve architectural designs in rural Indonesian communities.
